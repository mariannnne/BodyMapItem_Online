Project Description and Procedures:

You are being asked to take part in a research project conducted by Dr. Tor Wager, a Professor at the University of Colorado at Boulder's Institute for Cognitive Science and the Psychology and Neuroscience Department, 0447 UCB, Boulder, CO 80309-0447.

This study investigates personal emotional experiences, and how we thinking about emotions. During this study you will see many emotional photos (a total of 112). Some of these photos might be graphic or disturbing. Some photos contain explicit sexual content and nudity, therefore you may not want to complete this survey in a public space. While you view the photos we will ask you several questions about the emotions you are feeling. Please answer as honestly and completely as possible. Before beginning, please find a comfortable and private space in order to complete this study. Please also set aside about one hour to complete this survey.

Risks and Discomforts:

There are no known risks of participating. Participants may feel disturbed or embarrassed by the photos, but can quit the experiment at any time.

Benefits:

We do not foresee any significant benefits to you associated with participation.

Cost to Participant and Subject Payment:

There is no cost to you for participation in this study. You will be paid $0.50 for completion of the study.

Ending your participation:

You have the right to withdraw your consent or stop participating at any time. You have the right to refuse to answer any question(s) or participate in any procedure for any reason.

Confidentiality:

We will save your data indefinitely and may wish to use it in the future or share it with other researchers. We do not collect any identifying information about you, so the information we have will never be traceable to you.

Questions?

If you have questions regarding your rights as a participant, any concerns regarding this project or any dissatisfaction with any aspect of this study, you may report them -- confidentially, if you wish -- to the Institutional Review Board (IRB), 563 UCB, ARCE Room A15, University of Colorado at Boulder, Boulder, CO 80309, 303-735-3702.
